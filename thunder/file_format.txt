steps format:
reading from left to right
the first number is the keyboard char (its ASCII value) that was preesed. 
we or only saving the ship movement keys (a,w,d,x) and ship replace keys (b,s)
the second number is the time stamp of the key press

for your convenience, here is the ASCII values of the keys:
a - 97
w - 119
d - 100
x - 120
b - 98
s - 115


result format:
reading from left to right
the first number is the time stamp of the result 
the second number is the result
results are as follows:
0 - death, either by time or by overloading the ship
1 - level passed, both of the ships reached the end of the level
